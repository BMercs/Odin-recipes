Honey Garlic Porkchops

A sweet and delectable pan seared porkchop that no one can resist

    Ingredients

  • 6 1-inch thick porkchops
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 tablespoons Honey
  • 1/2 cup Ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

    Steps

  1. chop and dice the garlic
  2. mix garlic, Honey, Ketchup, and soy sauce in a mixing bowl to create the glaze
  3. season pork chop with salt and pepper
  4. heat pan in medium-high heat with oil
  5. add porkchops to pan and sear til fully cooked
  6. coat porkchops in glaze and sear into porkchops
