Brown Stew Chicken

Brown Stew Chicken is a Jamaican dish which features various flavoring. the main flavors that can be tasted from it are sweet and savory. The chicken has its distinct brown color and even though the name states it may be a stew, it actually is alot thicker and the stew portion is a sauce or gravy as we call it.

    Ingredients

  • 3 lbs chicken thigh, skinless
  • 2 teaspoons browning
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 yellow onion diced
  • 3 green onion sliced
  • 1 green pepper diced
  • 1 red pepper diced
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic chopped
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar

    Steps

  1. Combine chicken with browning, yellow onions, green onions, green and red peppers, garlic, onion and garlic powder, black pepper, and salt in a mixing bowl.
  2. mix and incorporate ingredients well in bowl
  3. wrap bowl and place in fridge for 3 hours to marinate
  4. heat stew pot with oil at the base to 300 degrees
  5. remove bowl from fridge and add to pot
  6. cook chicken until chicken begins to turn a white-ish color
  7. add in chicken broth, sugar and tomato paste
  8. stir to keep chicken from sticking to the bottom of the pot
  9. add salt to taste
  10. reduce heat and let simmer til the stock in the pot begins to thicken and become a sauce like texture
